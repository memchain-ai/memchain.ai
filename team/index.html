Meet Our Leadership Team

Visionary leaders with decades of combined experience in AI, enterprise technology, and product innovation, united by a mission to give AI the gift of memory.

Suresh Kalavala

CEO & Founder

Suresh Kalavala is a seasoned technology leader with over 27 years of experience driving innovation for Fortune 15 companies. A two-time startup founder and proven strategist, he brings deep expertise spanning AI/ML, enterprise architecture, cloud modernization, cybersecurity, and large-scale product development. In leadership roles including CTO for AI and Executive Partner at IBM, he has led global teams, delivered multi-cloud AI platforms, and implemented responsible AI solutions. His track record includes managing large enterprise transformations with substantial budgets and operational complexity, as well as founding a successful IoT startup in 2018, demonstrating an ability to operate effectively at both ends of the scale spectrum.

As the visionary behind MemChain AI, Suresh is building secure, scalable AI infrastructure to address real-world enterprise challenges. His hands-on technical leadership across AI/ML, cloud, cybersecurity, and enterprise architecture ensures MemChain AI is built on a rock-solid, future-proof foundation. With a career dedicated to advising C-suite executives, scaling high-performance global teams, and delivering enterprise-grade platforms with a focus on speed to market and long-term sustainability, Suresh brings a rare combination of deep technical acumen and strategic business judgment to every initiative he leads.

David Montgomery

Co-Founder & COO

David Montgomery is a C-level technology executive and AI strategist with a proven track record of leading large-scale technology delivery and building high-performing organizations across Fortune 100 enterprises. He has held senior leadership roles at IBM, Hewlett Packard Enterprise, Nationwide Insurance, and Cardinal Health, where he led enterprise PMOs, consulting and delivery practices, and architecture organizations supporting complex, multi-million-dollar transformations. His work has earned significant recognition, including two HPE Public Sector Executive of the Year awards and the National Black Engineer of the Year President's Award for IT. He has also led teams recognized by NASCIO for Agile Innovation and by IBM for Global Technology Excellence, and directed premier public sector accounts for HPE and Oracle in the U.S.

As Chief Operating Officer of MemChain AI, David drives operational execution, AI product delivery, and go-to-market scalability. He brings deep expertise in scaling technology solutions, executing mission-critical programs, and aligning business strategy with AI-driven innovation, honed across decades of work spanning enterprise and government sectors as an executive sponsor, program leader, and operator. David combines enterprise-scale execution discipline with startup agility, making him uniquely positioned to translate AI innovation into scalable, revenue-generating solutions.

Alberto F

Co-Founder & CTO

Alberto is a seasoned technologist with over 20 years of experience spanning AI infrastructure, Linux systems, cloud-native architecture, and security. He has led GPU-powered AI platform initiatives in partnership with NVIDIA, Run:AI, and Dell, architecting and deploying secure, high-performance environments powering real-world RAG, Co-Pilot, and agentic AI workloads across cloud and on-premises settings. Alberto is also a highly respected open-source contributor with a strong presence on Hugging Face, where he has published over 45 models and several large-scale datasets, including quantized Mistral variants and custom Llama-3 implementations fine-tuned using frameworks like Unsloth and TRL. His open-source portfolio reflects deep expertise in building scalable, production-ready AI systems that are widely adopted by the developer community.

At MemChain AI, Alberto plays a pivotal role as the CTO in shaping the platform's technical architecture, deployment strategy, and future-facing capabilities for agentic AI. He brings a rare combination of architectural vision and hands-on execution, drawing on extensive experience across product development, infrastructure security, DevOps, and applied AI prototyping. A trusted technical leader across support, product, and security teams, he thrives in high-pressure environments and consistently delivers scalable, secure system design. With roots in Argentina and a career that has taken him through Mexico, Brazil, and the United States, Alberto also brings a valuable cross-continental network spanning the Americas, opening strategic partnerships, talent pipelines, and go-to-market opportunities across the region.
